## Concept
Mirrorbound is a metaphorical visual showing what dissociation feels like and, likewise, how it feels when you "find resonance" through grounding; all of this is explored as a game of interactive motion.
Inspired by:
- Particle systems
- Generative art
- Personal experiences with mental health

## How It Works
The particles move as a mirror image of each other, but with a catch: the left side feels drained and slow while the right side feels energetic and flows freely. The goal is for the player to "find resonance" and "alignment" by bringing the particles to the center. The feedback responds with proximity and movement.
